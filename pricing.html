Frequently Asked Questions

How is data delivered to my S3 bucket?

We export your complete tracking data to your designated S3 bucket in real-time. You maintain full control and ownership of all data, with configurable export formats including JSON and structured datasets.

What makes SecurePixel tracking "exceptional"?

We capture micro-interactions that traditional tracking pixels miss - text selection, scroll velocity, form field completion patterns, and activity-based behavioral insights. Our architecture is built for the cookieless future.

How does GDPR compliance work?

GDPR compliance is built into our core architecture. We automatically redact PII, respect Do Not Track preferences, and provide complete data portability. No additional configuration required.

Can I integrate with my existing data warehouse?

Yes. Since we export to your S3 bucket, you can easily integrate with any data warehouse (Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift) or BI tool. Your data, your infrastructure.

How does pricing work for seasonal traffic spikes?

Overages are calculated monthly at $10 per 100K additional pageviews. You're only charged for what you use above your plan limit, making it predictable and fair.

What's included in implementation assistance?

For Growth and Enterprise plans, we provide setup guidance, custom event configuration, and integration support to ensure you're capturing the right insights from day one.
